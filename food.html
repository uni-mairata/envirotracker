The Impact of Food

The way food is produced heavily correlates with the environmental impact that it has. From growing crops to processing, transporting, selling, storing and throwing away food â€“ everything we eat has an impact on the environment and the climate.

There are a few ways to reduce our environemental impact through our food choices, such as:

  • Buying less red meat (red meat is a MAJOR issue and has multiple negative impacts, like increasing the emission of gasses that contribute to climate change)

  • Reduce the amount of food that is wasted

  • Choosing to buy more locally grown produce

  • Eat fewer foods that use lots of water to produce, like tea, beef, chocolate (cocoa tree), dairy products, rice, etc.
